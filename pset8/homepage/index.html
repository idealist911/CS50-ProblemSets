My Homepage

Welcome

I am a Physics teacher and I thought that I should create a website to help teachers in their job. In particular, a clock would be helpful for tests to show students the time in case there is no clock in the classroom.

TBH, I am a noob at web programming and these are some of the resources that have helped me:

  • Carol Skelly in her article -- helped with the navigation bar (because W3 schools' solution did not work well for me)
  • freeCodeCamp in their article -- helped with creating a hyperlink that opens on a new tab
  • Shaif Arfan on his YouTube Channel -- helped with creating an analog clock
  • Sofia Gallo in her article -- helped with her views on what makes a website accessible to screen readers
  • W3 schools -- helped with various things on HTML, CSS, and Javascript, in particular, on the navigation bar

⚒ This website is yet to be completed. Perhaps next time I might add a carousel or cards to highlight some of the features of the website on the front page.
